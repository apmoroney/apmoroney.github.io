Senior Product (UX / UI) Designer

Description

This position is focused on the end user while working collaboratively with the product managers and engineers. The main focus is on the user experience where the design of the product is critical. This role seems ideal because it is somewhat varied itself. It is not solely focused on the technical side of the project, but rather the project's impact on others. Therefore research into the user groups would be required in order to meet the requirements the project is designed to fulfil. This sort of role interests me because of the idea of starting from design to prototype, to finally seeing the finished product post-development.
